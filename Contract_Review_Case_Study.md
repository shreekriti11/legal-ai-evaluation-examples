Contract Review Case Study
Document Type- Non-Disclosure Agreement (NDA)
Key Clause Reviewed- Confidentiality Obligations
Potential Risk Identified-The clause did not clearly define the duration of confidentiality obligations, creating ambiguity regarding post-termination responsibilities.
Suggested Revision- Include a provision specifying that confidentiality obligations survive termination of the agreement for a defined period, such as three to five years.
Legal Rationale- Clear survival clauses reduce ambiguity, strengthen enforceability, and provide certainty regarding the parties' continuing obligations.
Conclusion-The agreement would benefit from clearer drafting to ensure enforceability and reduce potential disputes regarding confidential information.
